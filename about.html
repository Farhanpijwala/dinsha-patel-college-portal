About DPCCS

Our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence in education

Our History

Founded in 1995, DPCCS began as a small college with just three departments and 200 students. Today, we've grown into a comprehensive university with over 15,000 students across eight faculties.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation in teaching and research, always staying true to our founding principles of academic excellence, inclusivity, and service to society.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide transformative educational experiences that prepare students to be ethical leaders and engaged citizens in a global society.

We envision a world where education breaks down barriers and creates opportunities for all. By fostering innovation, critical thinking, and cultural understanding, we aim to be a catalyst for positive change.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards in teaching, research, and service.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, fairness, and ethical behavior in all we do.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and discovery to solve complex problems.
  • Diversity: We value and respect differences in people and ideas.
  • Community: We foster collaboration and engagement within and beyond our campus.
