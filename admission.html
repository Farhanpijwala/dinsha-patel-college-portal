Admission Requirements

High School Diploma

Official transcripts showing successful completion of high school or equivalent GED certification.

Standardized Tests

SAT or ACT scores required. Average SAT: 1200-1400, Average ACT: 24-30.

GPA Requirements

Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale. Competitive applicants have 3.5+.

Letters of Recommendation

Two letters from teachers or counselors who can speak to your academic abilities.

Personal Essay

A 500-650 word essay showcasing your personality, goals, and why you chose our college.

Application Fee

Non-refundable application fee of $50. Fee waivers available for qualifying students.
